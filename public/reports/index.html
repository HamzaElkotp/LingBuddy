Feedback and correction

Band:6.5 Overall, the essay provides a clear description of the changes that occurred in the road access to a city hospital over a three-year period. However, there are some grammar and spelling mistakes, as well as some areas that could be improved. Suggestions: - Begin with an introductory sentence that clearly states what the essay is about. - Use more precise language to describe the changes, such as "two roundabouts were added" instead of "there are two roundabouts were added." - Use more specific language to describe the location of the bus station and car parks, such as "the new bus station was built at the intersection of the Ring road and Hospital road" and "the new car park for public visitors was established on the east side of the hospital, with an entrance from the Ring road." - Avoid using repetitive language, such as using "staff" twice in the same sentence. - Use more accurate verb tenses, such as "the hospital building remained unchanged" instead of "the hospital build is the only thing that did not change." Grammar and spelling mistakes: - "there is a noticed change happened" should be "a noticeable change occurred." - "were added to them" could be "were added at these locations." - "car park was for both of the staff and the public visitors" could be "car park was for both staff and public visitors." - "staffs’ cars" should be "staff's cars." - "Equally important" should be "Similarly" or "Additionally." - "to allow just public to park their cars there" should be "to allow only the public to park their cars there."
